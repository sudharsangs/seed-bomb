What are Seed Bombs?

Seed bombs are the seeds packed in a compressed bundle of clay and nutrient rich compost.

Why Seed Bombs?

Seed bombing is the easiest and the best way to cultivate the plant and trees.If you scatter native plant seeds, they are often eaten up by insects, birds, and rodents or washed away by the rain-but these seeds are protected inside a ball. When it rains, the clay in the ball holds the water and helps the seeds to germinate. When the seeds germinate, the composthelps the seedlings grow.

How to use Seed Bombs?

Ideal place for Seed bombing:

Forests, Vacant sites, farm lands, empty grounds, gardens and your own yards or pots.
